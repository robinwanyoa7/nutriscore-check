About NutriScore

NutriScore is a lightweight browser extension that surfaces quick, readable nutrition hints while you shop online. It overlays a simple grade badge on products and provides estimated nutrient details to help you make healthier choices faster.

Provide a brief health profile to get personalized suggestions and tailored guidance directly in your shopping flow.

Set up my health profile

Key features

Everything you need to make smarter food choices while you shop — without leaving the page.

Grade badges

Compact A–E score badges appear on each product card as you hover.

Nutrient overlay

Click a badge to see calories, fat, sugar, salt, fiber, and protein estimates.

Instant scan

Lightweight content script runs entirely in your browser — no data sent anywhere.

Compare at a glance

Quickly compare multiple products side by side as you browse.

How to Install NutriScore Extension

Get NutriScore running in under two minutes.

  • Install the Extension — Click on the Extensions icon (the puzzle piece) in your browser's toolbar, then select Manage Extensions. — Use the search bar to look up NutriScore Checkout Tool and click Install.
  • Confirm Installation — Once installed, the NutriScore icon should appear in your browser's toolbar. If it's hidden, click the puzzle piece icon and pin NutriScore for easy access.
  • Activate on a Shopping Page — Navigate to any supported shopping or checkout page. Click the NutriScore icon in your toolbar to open the popup, then click Activate.
  • Start Using NutriScore — Once activated, NutriScore will have and icon pop-up, that will be hovering over the product. that is interactable by a mouse hover.
