NutriScore

Quick nutritional hints on shopping pages

What this extension does

NutriScore scans product listings on shopping pages, adds a compact grade badge to each product, and shows a small overlay with an estimated nutritional breakdown when you click the badge. The goal is to help shoppers quickly compare products while browsing.

Key features

  • Injects NutriScore badges onto product cards when you hover over them.
  • Shows an overlay with calories, fat, sugar, salt, fiber and protein estimates.
  • Uses simple heuristics and a small scraper to find product titles and prices.
  • Lightweight, runs entirely in the browser as a content script.

How it works

The content script (`content.js`) calls `scrapeProducts()` from `scraper.js` to locate product containers, creates badges via `iconLayer.js`, and shows details using `overlayLayer.js` + `nutrientBar.js`. Tooltips and the activation prompt are provided by `tooltip.js` and `activatePrompt.js`.

Install (developer)

  1. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions.
  2. Enable "Developer mode" then click "Load unpacked".
  3. Select this project's folder (the folder containing manifest.json).

After loading, visit a shopping page and click the extension popup to activate NutriScore.
